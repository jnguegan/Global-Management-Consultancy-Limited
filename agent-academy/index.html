Course Overview

Agent Academy is built to help users understand the regulatory framework, study efficiently, practise actively, and move step by step toward exam readiness.

What the academy includes

  • 1 A structured multilingual playbook covering the core FIFA football agent exam themes.
  • 2 Topic-based quizzes linked directly to regulations and study modules.
  • 3 A dashboard-based learning environment with plan-based access and progress pathways.
  • 4 Mock exam preparation designed to simulate a serious revision experience.

What You Will Study

Core modules aligned with the academy structure.

The public overview introduces the scope of the academy. Once users create an account, they can move into the dashboard and begin studying according to their plan.

FFAR 01

FFAR Core Principles

Understand the purpose, licensing framework, structure and practical foundations of the FIFA Football Agent Regulations.

Contracts 02

Representation Agreements

Study written form, signatures, duration, validity requirements, scope of services, exclusivity and related legal risks.

Fees 03

Service Fees and Commissions

Review service fee rules, fee caps, who may pay, instalments, and when agents are not entitled to fees not yet due.

Protection 04

Minors

Understand timing restrictions, guardian involvement, enforceability and the enhanced safeguards required for dealings involving minors.

Integrity 05

Conflicts of Interest

Learn disclosure rules, dual representation limits, prohibited combinations and practical conflict-management principles.

Football Rules 06

RSTP, Governance, Ethics and Disciplinary

Go beyond agent regulations and revise the broader football framework tested across the exam syllabus.
