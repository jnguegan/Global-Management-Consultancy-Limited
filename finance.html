Global Sport Finance

Premium financial infrastructure for the international football ecosystem.

Global Sport Finance is a specialised financial services platform provided by Global Management Consultancy Limited, designed to help football clubs, agencies, players and internationally mobile stakeholders manage money efficiently across borders.

Built specifically for the international football industry, it combines multi-currency accounts, foreign exchange, international payments, payout workflows and card-based expense management within one structured solution delivered through regulated financial partnerships.
